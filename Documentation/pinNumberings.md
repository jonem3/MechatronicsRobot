# Pin Numberings
## Batteries:

Battery + 7.2v - Vin

Battery 0v - Gnd

## Encoders:

### Left:

Encoder Green - 10

Encoder Blue - 11

### Right:

Encoder Green - 12

Encoder Blue - 13

## Distance IR Sensors

Right IR - A8

Left IR - A9

## Arm:

Shoulder Motor - 44

Elbow Motor - 45

## Motors:

StandBy - 9

### Left motor:
I1 - 7

I2 - 8

PWM - 5


### Right motor:
I1 - 2

I2 - 3

PWM - 4
